Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist 

* Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone, even if they say they are from your bank or customer support. Genuine services will never ask for it.
* Keep this in mind: you don’t need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money. If you’re entering a PIN, you are actually sending money.
* Take a moment to check UPI requests carefully: before approving them, especially “Collect Request” notifications. Don’t rush.
* Be careful with links you receive: on SMS, WhatsApp, or email. If something looks suspicious or asks for your details, it’s better not to click it.
* Use strong, different passwords: for your accounts and turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) for extra security.
* Download apps only from trusted sources: like the Google Play Store or Apple App Store to avoid fake or harmful apps.
* Stay alert while buying or selling online: If someone is rushing you or offering deals that seem too good to be true, take a step back.
* Don’t share personal details: like Aadhaar number, bank info, or passwords on unknown websites or with unverified people.
* Always log out after using shared devices: and avoid checking important accounts on public computers or open Wi-Fi.
* Keep an eye on your bank transactions regularly: and enable alerts so you can quickly spot anything unusual.
